Key Features

Things the Website Can Do

These aren't just design touches — every feature below serves a real, practical purpose for your clinic and your patients.

📅
Online Bookings

Patients Can Book Appointments Directly

There's a booking form right on the website. Patients fill in their name, phone number, email, which treatment they want, and pick a date and time. The form checks for mistakes (like a wrong phone number or missing email) and won't let them submit until everything looks right. Once they book, you receive a proper email notification with all their details. No phone tag. No missed calls. Bookings come to you automatically.

🤖
AI Chatbot

A 24/7 AI Assistant Called "Dr. Byte"

There's a chat bubble in the corner of every page. Patients can click it and chat with an AI assistant that knows all about your clinic's services. It can answer questions like "How much does whitening cost?" or "Do you do braces?" It can also walk a patient through booking an appointment step by step — collecting their name, phone, email, treatment choice, and preferred date — and send the booking to you as an email. It works all night long, even when your team is closed. It can even speak its replies out loud for patients who prefer listening over reading.

💰
Cost Calculator

Instant Treatment Price Estimator

On the Pricing page, patients can tick the treatments they're interested in and the website automatically calculates a total cost estimate — including taxes — in real time. If they like what they see, they click "Book This Package" and the website takes them straight to the booking form with their chosen treatments already filled in. Patients who see clear pricing are far more likely to book.

📊
Live Dashboard

A Live Appointments Display on the Homepage

The homepage has a live-looking admin panel that shows recent appointment requests in real time. When someone books — either through the form or via the chatbot — a new entry appears on this panel with the patient name, treatment, and time. The counters (total bookings, active leads, chat sessions) all update automatically. This shows visitors that the clinic is active and in demand, which builds trust instantly.

🖼️
Before & After

Drag-to-Compare Smile Transformations

On the Doctors page, there are three side-by-side comparison photos showing actual patient smile transformations — Teeth Whitening, Aligners, and Dental Implants. Visitors grab a slider handle and drag it left or right to reveal the before and after. Seeing real results is the most powerful way to convince a potential patient to book.

📈
Social Proof

Patient Testimonials & Statistics

The homepage features a rotating testimonials section where happy patients share their experience. It also displays the clinic's key numbers — 12,000+ patients treated, 98% satisfaction rate, 15+ specialists — which all count up dramatically when the visitor scrolls to that section. These kinds of numbers and stories make a hesitant patient say "okay, I trust this place."

What Was Used to Build It

The Tools Behind the Website

You don't need to understand any of this — but if a client or collaborator ever asks "how was this built?", here's a simple answer for each tool.

🏗️
HTML & CSS — The Building Blocks

HTML is like the walls and rooms of the website. CSS is the paint, furniture, and décor. Together they define what the site looks like and how it's laid out.

⚡
JavaScript — The Brain

This is the invisible logic that makes things happen — like the chatbot responding, the calculator adding up prices, the booking form checking your details, and the dashboard updating live.

📧
EmailJS — Booking Notifications

A service that delivers the patient's booking details straight to an email inbox the moment they submit the form — without needing a separate server or backend system.

🔤
Google Fonts — Premium Typography

The clean, modern text style you see across the website comes from two professional fonts (Outfit and Plus Jakarta Sans) loaded for free from Google's font library.

🗣️
Browser Voice Engine — Chatbot Speaks

Modern browsers have a built-in text-to-speech engine. The website uses it so the AI chatbot can read its replies out loud when the patient turns on the voice option — no extra service needed.

💾
Browser Memory — Remembers Preferences

The website remembers things like whether you prefer dark mode or light mode, and can carry your selected treatments from the Pricing page to the Booking form automatically. All saved locally on your device.
